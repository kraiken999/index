Folding Map Fi

A folding map is a composition of elementary folds: 

Fi = R(li,1, Θi,1) ∘ R(li,2, Θi,2) ∘ … ∘ R(li,Ki, Θi,Ki)

On each face of the crease subdivision, Fi is a rigid motion in ℝ³ (piecewise isometry).
