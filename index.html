Folding Geometry into Quantum Physics

A framework exploring how origami-based geometric folding can encode quantum states and spacetime structure.

About the Project

Quantum Origami is an ongoing theoretical physics project connecting modular origami geometry with quantum mechanics and quantum gravity.

Core Ideas

Folded Configuration Space

Origami crease patterns define discrete configuration spaces that can be mapped to quantum state spaces.

Amplitude as Fold Density

Fold density and layer multiplicity encode probability amplitudes and interference patterns.

Curvature and Gravity

Local curvature induced by folding provides a geometric analogue of spacetime curvature in gravity.
